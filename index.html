Welcome to Malcolm X Secondary School

At Malcolm X Secondary School, we are dedicated to fostering a nurturing environment where students can excel academically, socially, and personally. Academics

At Malcolm X Secondary School, we pride ourselves on offering a robust and diverse curriculum designed to foster academic excellence and personal growth. Catering to students from Senior 1 (S.1) to Senior 6 (S.6), our programs are tailored to meet the educational needs and aspirations of every learner.

Lower Secondary (S.1 - S.4)

At Malcolm X Secondary School, our Lower Secondary program aims to provide a strong foundation for academic success and personal development. We offer a comprehensive curriculum that includes core subjects, electives, and a variety of co-curricular activities to foster a well-rounded education.

Upper Secondary (S.5 - S.6)

Our Upper Secondary program is designed to prepare students for higher education and future career opportunities. Malcolm X Secondary School offers specialized streams in Science, Arts, and Technical subjects, allowing students to tailor their education to their interests and aspirations.

Co-Curricular Activities

At Malcolm X Secondary School, we believe in nurturing well-rounded individuals. Our co-curricular activities offer students the opportunity to explore their interests, develop new skills, and build lasting friendships. From sports and clubs to cultural activities and community service, there's something for everyone.
